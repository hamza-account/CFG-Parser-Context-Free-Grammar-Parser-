Grammar Rules (Unambiguous CFG)

E → E + T | E - T | T
T → T * F | T / F | F
F → ( E ) | number

E (Expression) handles + − with left-associativity.
T (Term) handles × ÷, giving them higher precedence.
F (Factor) handles atoms and grouped sub-expressions.
Precedence: ( ) > * / > + -
